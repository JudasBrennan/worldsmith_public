## Notes

- Retrograde rotation (spins backwards relative to orbit)
- Extreme greenhouse effect: 231 K blackbody → 737 K surface
- Most circular orbit of any planet (e = 0.0068)
- Near-twin of Earth in size/mass but radically different atmosphere
- No moons
