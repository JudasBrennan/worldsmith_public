## Notes

- Saturn's density (687 kg/m³) is less than water — the only planet with this property
- Like Jupiter, tidal Q is uncertain. WorldSmith uses Q = 10⁵ for gas giant parents
- Rotation period 10.656 hr used in WorldSmith's recession calculation
