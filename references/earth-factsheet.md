## Notes

- Geothermal flux 0.087 W/m² (often rounded to 0.09 W/m²) is the reference
  value used in WorldSmith's tidal heating normalisation
- Bond albedo 0.306 is the CERES-derived value; older sources use 0.29–0.31
- Greenhouse effect raises surface from 254 K blackbody to 288 K mean
