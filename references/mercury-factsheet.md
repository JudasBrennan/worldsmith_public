## Notes

- Highest density of any planet (5.43 g/cm³) — large iron core
- 3:2 spin-orbit resonance (not tidally locked)
- Highest orbital eccentricity of any planet (0.206)
- Extreme day/night temperature swing (~600 K)
- No moons, no rings
