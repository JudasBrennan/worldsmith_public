## Notes

- Recession rate 3.83 cm/yr is the key calibration target for WorldSmith's
  tidal recession calculation
- Sidereal period = rotation period (synchronous/tidally locked)
- The Moon's density (3.34 g/cm³) places it in WorldSmith's "Rocky" composition
  class (μ = 50 GPa, Q = 30)
