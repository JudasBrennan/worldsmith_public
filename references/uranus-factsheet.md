## Notes

- Extreme axial tilt (97.77°) — essentially rolls around its orbit
- Retrograde rotation (defined by IAU convention due to >90° tilt)
- Lowest minimum temperature of any planet (49 K)
- Wind speeds up to 900 km/h
- Ice giant: ~80% water, methane, ammonia ices
